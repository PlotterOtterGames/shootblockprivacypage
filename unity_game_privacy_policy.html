Privacy Policy for Shoot Block

Last updated: May 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Shoot Block mobile game ("the App").

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information from users of Shoot Block.

However, our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob, which may collect and process certain information as described below.

Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

Shoot Block uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, mobile network information
  • Ad Interaction Data: Which ads you view or tap
  • App Usage Information: How you interact with the app
  • Approximate Location: General location based on IP address for relevant ads
  • Advertising ID: Device advertising identifier for personalized ads
Important: All advertising data is collected and processed by Google AdMob according to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this data.

How Information is Used

The information collected by AdMob is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad effectiveness
  • Improve advertising services
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Your Choices

You can control personalized advertising by:

  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off Personalized Ads

You can also reset your advertising ID in your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information because we do not collect any. AdMob may share information with other Google services and third-party partners as outlined in Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

Google AdMob has additional protections for children as outlined in their privacy policies.

International Data Transfers

AdMob may transfer and process data in countries other than your own. Google maintains appropriate safeguards for international data transfers as described in their privacy policies.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

For more information about how Google handles data collected through AdMob, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, if necessary, providing notice within the App.

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. For any data collected by AdMob, you can contact Google directly or use the controls mentioned in the "Your Choices" section above.
